Process

My Design Approach
Step 01

Discovery & Research

Understanding your goals, audience, and context through detailed conversations and market research to establish project foundations.

Step 02

Concept & Sketching

Developing initial concepts through sketches and mood boards to explore visual directions and establish the creative approach.

Step 03

Design Development

Refining selected concepts into polished designs, creating iterations based on feedback, and preparing for presentation.

Step 04

Delivery & Support

Preparing final files, providing style guides if needed, and ensuring you have everything required for implementation.
